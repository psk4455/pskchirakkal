Pravasi Seva Kendram (Pravasis Limited) is an authorised NORKA Roots service centre in Kannur, Kerala. We bridge the gap between Malayali expatriates and the Kerala Government's welfare schemes — making it easy to register, subscribe, and avail benefits from anywhere in the world.

Beyond NRI services, we are a full-service business centre offering government certificate assistance, DTP, printing, exam registrations, travel ticketing, visa processing, and financial services.
